2048

2048 is a sliding puzzle game where you combine matching tiles to create a tile with the number 2048. Slide tiles in any direction and merge them to reach the goal.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple two-player game where players take turns marking spaces on a 3x3 grid with either an "X" or an "O". The first player to get three of their marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins the game.

Snake

Snake is a classic game where you control a snake to eat food and grow longer. Avoid running into walls or your own tail to keep playing!

Pong

A simple yet classic arcade game where two players control paddles and try to bounce a ball back and forth. The objective is to score points by making the opponent miss the ball.

Flappy Bird

Tap the screen to make the bird fly and avoid the pipes. The goal is to get through as many pipes as possible without hitting them.

Agar.io

Control a small cell and consume others to grow larger. Avoid being eaten by larger cells and aim to become the largest cell in the game.

Among Us

Among Us is a multiplayer social deduction game where players work together on a spaceship to complete tasks. However, some players are secretly impostors who try to sabotage the mission and eliminate the crew. Players must identify and vote off the impostors before it’s too late.

Slither.io

Slither.io is a multiplayer online game where players control a snake and compete to grow longer by eating pellets. The goal is to become the largest snake on the server while avoiding other snakes. If you collide with another snake, you lose the game.

Paper.io

Paper.io is a multiplayer online game where players control a colored block that leaves a trail behind as it moves. The objective is to capture territory by creating loops and expanding your area while avoiding collisions with other players' trails. The player with the most territory at the end of the game wins.
